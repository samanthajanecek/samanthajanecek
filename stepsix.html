step six.

Accept It. When death comes to those you love, mourn them. Roar with anger and sink into the great cavern of sadness that opens up but, please, remember that this is natural. It is only death. Death cannot dissolve love. It has no power to steal joy or memories. Speak of the dead often and without sorrow or solemnity. Let them be as present in your words and thoughts as they always were. Laugh, smile and tell their stories. Cry. Think of them, pray for them, speak to them, remember them..
