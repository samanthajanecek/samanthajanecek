how to make friends

step five.

Let It Become Familiar. Death exists whether you speak about it five times a day or five times in a lifetime. Not taking about it doesn’t make it go away. Talking about death pulls it from the shadows and helps us become familiar with it. The shock value is lost. When death arrives, it will appear as a familiar friend. Be honest with loved ones and children. They will die and so will you and that’s okay. Be frank and don’t stumble in your words. Let death become as present as life..
